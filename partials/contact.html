About us

Emiljano Alizoti

began apprenticeship under a master stone mason at the age 14 in Bologna, Italy. His extensive construction experience covers tile and stone, concrete, carpentry, roofing and stucco. Not only is he a skilled craftsman, but he has an eye for functional space design. He often assists owners making design decisions.

Taba Alizoti

graduated from Arizona State University with a BS degree in Construction. She has worked for general contractors as well as the Safeway grocery chains as a construction project manager in Metro Phoenix area before moving to San Francisco.

Professional Partner: Dennis Martin, D M Custom Homes, Inc.

Dennis Martin has built quality custom homes in the Bay Area since 1983. Alizoti Co. has been working with D M Custom Homes, Inc. since 2008.

Dennis and Emiljano quickly found out that they were a good match, because the two companies shared the same work ethic which is to have respect for everyone involved, and work as a team to build quality products. They have developed a great working relationship over the last few years.

Now Dennis is retiring from running a full-time construction business, but, not ready to quit all at once, he decided to collaborate with Alizoti Co., and share his 50 years of expertise in custom home building. Dennis acts as a construction consultant.
