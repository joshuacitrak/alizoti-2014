About us

Emiljano Alizoti

began apprenticeship under a master stone mason at the age 14 in Bologna, Italy. His extensive construction experience covers tile and stone, concrete, carpentry, roofing and stucco. Not only is he a skilled craftsman, but he has an eye for functional space design. He often assists owners making design decisions.

Taba Alizoti

graduated from Arizona State University with a BS degree in Construction. She has worked for general contractors as well as the Safeway grocery chains as a construction project manager in Metro Phoenix area before moving to San Francisco.

Emiljano and Taba

have a combined 30 plus years of experience in the construction and contracting business field. While Emiljano runs the field-work, Taba corresponds and connects with current and potential clients regarding proposals, estimates, orders and billing. As a team, they have vast knowledge in all phases of project development, which allows they to walk with each client through the entire build process, explaining each step in a clear and straightforward manner.

Local, Family Owned

Emiljano and Taba have over 30 years experience contracting for your neighbors in San Francisco, the North Bay and Pennisula giving the quality of service you'd expect from a large, out of town contracting firm in the package of a small, local family business.

Licensed, Bonded

Alizoti Co. is licensed and bonded by the California Contractors State License Board (CCSLB) license #899785 for B-General Building Contractor & C54-Ceramic and Mosiac tile.
